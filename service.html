E-VAULTING

         Electronic vaulting, on the other hand, always entails transferring data via a network from a primary site to a secondary (secure) one. Depending on the information and business objectives, electronic vaulting (e-vaulting) is an alternative to tape backup to ensure that their services are up to date. E-vaulting is gaining in popularity for a wide range of businesses because to the massive volumes of data that must be handled, constant needs, and competitive pressure.



ONLINE RECORD MANGEMENT SYSTEM

         Diamond Hill Record Center offered service of online record management system that may be used to manage an organization's complete business records or to create a digital archive for a specific set of data. The organization will not have to spend in hardcopy or softcopy material, and the records will be available only through a secure software system at any time and from any location. Even in the case of a fire or crisis, company records must be safeguarded at all times


DIGITAL SERVICE

         Scanning and storage are two of the services supplied by the records management company. Diamond Hill Record Center uses cutting-edge technologies to provide end-to-end digitization services, including document collection, indexing, digitalization, and archiving. In addition, the Diamond Hill Record Center offers comprehensive physical management services at the document, carton, and file levels.

This division also organized IT (Information Technology) literacy and awareness programs using a variety of techniques, including brochure/pamphlet distribution, demonstrations, software and hardware promotions, and advisory service visits.
